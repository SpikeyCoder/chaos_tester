Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our audit tool

Is the website audit really free?
Yes, our website audit tool is completely free to use. There are no hidden charges, no premium tiers, and no credit card required. You can audit as many websites as you want.
Do I need to create an account?
No account is needed. Simply enter your website URL and click "Audit Website" to get your comprehensive report instantly. No signup, no email verification.
What does the audit check?
Our audit covers SEO (meta tags, headings, broken links), security (SSL, headers, vulnerabilities), performance (load speed, resource optimization), accessibility (WCAG compliance), forms validation, and AI visibility across platforms like ChatGPT, Perplexity, and Claude.
How long does an audit take?
Most audits complete in under 30 seconds for small to medium websites. Larger sites with hundreds of pages may take a few minutes. You can monitor progress in real-time.
Can I audit any website?
You can audit any publicly accessible website. For sites behind authentication, you can provide login credentials in the advanced options to audit protected pages as well.
How often should I audit my website?
We recommend auditing your website at least once a month, or after making significant changes. Regular audits help catch issues before they impact your search rankings or user experience.
