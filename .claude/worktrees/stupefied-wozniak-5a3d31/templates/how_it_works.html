How It Works

Three steps. Under a minute. No tech skills needed.

Step 1: Paste Your Website Address

Go to the home page and type your website URL into the box. Any public website works -- your business site, a blog, or an online store.

Want more control? Click "Customize Audit Modules" to change settings like how deep to check, how many pages to scan, and scan speed.

Step 2: Pick What to Check

By default we run a Basic Scan that covers the things most businesses care about. You can also turn on extra checks:

  • Page Availability -- Can visitors reach every page on your site?
  • Broken Links -- Are there any dead links that lead nowhere?
  • Forms -- Do your contact forms and sign-up forms work correctly?
  • Security -- Is your site safe from common attacks?
  • Error Handling -- Does your site recover when something goes wrong?

All basic checks are on by default. Just click "Audit Website" and we handle the rest.

Step 3: Read Your Report

In about 30 seconds you get a clear report. It shows your top issues first, sorted by impact. For each issue you will see:

  • Which page has the problem
  • How serious it is (critical, warning, or just a tip)
  • What went wrong, in plain language
  • What to do about it, step by step

You can share the report with your web developer, download it, or come back to it later.
