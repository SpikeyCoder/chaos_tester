About Website Auditor

Website Auditor is built by Armstrong HoldCo LLC. We created this free website audit tool to help small business owners understand and improve their web presence.

Our Mission

To help every small business have a healthier, more discoverable, and more secure web presence.

What We Do

Website Auditor provides comprehensive analysis of your website across multiple dimensions:

  • SEO Analysis: Identify on-page SEO issues and optimization opportunities
  • Security Scanning: Detect common security vulnerabilities and misconfigurations
  • Broken Links Detection: Find and report broken internal and external links
  • Performance Metrics: Understand your site's speed and performance characteristics
  • AI Visibility: See how well your site performs in AI search indexes
  • Business Intelligence: Detect and analyze embedded business information

All scans are performed instantly and reports are provided free of charge.
